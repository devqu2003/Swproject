Our Story

Gateau was established on nov 05 /2020 We the owners of
Gateau Cafe were motivated to start our
business based on our own personal experiences
with dining out. We enjoyed going
out to eat with our spouses
and valued quality over quantity. We noticed
a gap in the Cafe industry in Addis,
Ethiopia, and saw an opportunity to fill
that gap by starting our own cafe.


Starting a Cafe can be a challenging but rewarding
endeavor. It requires careful planning, a
strong understanding of the local market, and a
willingness to work hard to create a
unique and appealing dining experience
for customers. It's important to conduct research
and create a solid business plan before launching
a new restaurant, and to be prepared to adapt
and make changes as needed to
stay competitive and meet the needs of customers.
